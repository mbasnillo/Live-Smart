LIVE SMART

 

What is Live Smart?

Live Smart is a carbon analytics web application that allows a person to compute for their individual carbon footprint levels and view their previous computations.

View graphs and statistics and see how much people know about global warming and carbon footprints.

View your carbon footprint levels over a period of time.

Mobile responsive so that you can easily compute on your phone as well.
