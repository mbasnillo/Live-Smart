LIVE SMART

 

What is Live Smart?

Live Smart is a web application that allows a person to compute for their individual carbon footprint levels and view their previous computations, and aims to gather and display data on people's knowledge on carbon footprints and climate change. It also seeks to provide resources and information regarding the topic in order to raise awareness on this particular topic.

View graphs and statistics and see how much people know about global warming and carbon footprints.

View your carbon footprint levels over a period of time.

Mobile responsive so that you can easily compute on your phone or tablet as well.
